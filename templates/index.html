About

Founded by a group of six university students, Hoot Housing was born out of a frustration with off-campus student housing rentals that were less than ideal. By creating this site we hope to create a community of collective wisdom built through individual experiences, and give students confidence when committing to their new homes.

Take a look - read or write a reviews - and lets change the community of student housing together.

Find Your Home

Hoot Housing is an online review site that provides a place for university students to view, rate, and review their housing experiences. Users will be able to search for reviews by address or keyword in order to browse the knowledge of past tenants and find the home most suitable for them.

Leave A Review

Feel like joining conversation and helping out your fellow students? If you have a recent housing experience (good or bad!) we want to hear about it!
